Draffan Wellness: A Doctor-Led Wellness Brand Changing Daily Health Habits

Draffan Wellness is a health and wellness brand founded by Dr. Affan Qaiser, focused on bringing doctor-formulated natural supplements to everyday consumers, primarily in Pakistan.

Who Is Behind Draffan Wellness?

At its core, Draffan Wellness aims to bridge the gap between modern medicine and natural care. The brand is led by Dr. Affan Qaiser, a consultant gastroenterologist and transplant hepatologist, who uses his clinical experience to guide product development.

His expertise informs formulations designed for real-world needs such as fatigue, low stamina, digestive health, and stress management.

Mission & Philosophy

The company’s mission is to offer safe, effective, and evidence-based health solutions that feel accessible and trustworthy. Draffan Wellness focuses on long-term wellness rather than temporary fixes, grounding its products in science and clinical expertise.

What Does Draffan Wellness Offer?

  • Men’s Vitality: A daily supplement designed to support energy, stamina, and overall male wellness using herbal and nutrient-rich ingredients.
  • Magnesium Glycinate: A mineral supplement formulated to support calm energy, muscle relaxation, and balanced living.
  • Liver Ease, Joint Ease & Hair Care: Targeted blends addressing detox support, joint comfort, and hair strength.

All products are natural, halal-certified, and free from harsh chemicals, reflecting the brand’s commitment to clean wellness solutions.

How Draffan Wellness Supplements Work

Draffan Wellness formulas combine traditional herbal ingredients with essential vitamins and minerals to support everyday health.

For example, the Men’s Vitality supplement features botanicals such as Horny Goat Weed, Maca Root, and Panax Ginseng, along with zinc and vitamin E, to help support energy, endurance, and daily performance.

The brand emphasizes consistent, long-term use alongside a balanced lifestyle to achieve noticeable benefits such as improved focus, reduced fatigue, and sustained energy levels.

Quality & Safety Standards

According to the brand, all supplements are manufactured in facilities that meet strict safety and quality standards. Products are suitable for daily use, vegetarian-friendly, halal-certified, and made with responsibly sourced ingredients.

Consumer Considerations

As with anyDr Affan Wellness supplement brand, consumers are encouraged to verify product authenticity and consult healthcare professionals before starting new supplements, especially if they have existing medical conditions or are taking medication.

Individual results may vary, making informed decision-making and professional guidance an important part of any wellness journey.
